Angell Jackson Kennedy, MBA, Esq., is an exceptional attorney and marketing strategist who built her business career on the foundation she obtained through her education at Xavier University of Louisiana where she graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and Marketing.

For the last 5 years at East Baton Rouge Council on Aging (EBRCOA), Angell developed a unified digital transformation and branding strategy to enhance public awareness and engagement, achieving significant growth and national media coverage. The strategic initiatives positioned EBRCOA as a leader in senior services, boosting its profile and fostering strong relationships with stakeholders. Through targeted content and digital marketing, Angell ensured cohesive branding across all channels, resulting in increased engagement and visibility within the community and beyond.
